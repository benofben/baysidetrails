Get Involved

Original Content (Unabridged)

We periodically organize walks of the trails.

Previously we had done a bunch of work parties. In those we cleaned up garbage, fixed up the trail and cut vines.

Parks Tacoma owns the Garfield Gulch section of the trail. They are not allowing work parties anymore. We’re trying to figure out how to work with the city to get those going again.

Social Media

We’re on Facebook. We post events to this page: https://www.facebook.com/groups/baysidetrails/
